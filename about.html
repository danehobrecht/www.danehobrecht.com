About

Sometimes, when I'm acting, I get the idea that it’s me doing it. The reality is that if I make you smile, laugh, cry, or love, you’re doing so with God. My job is just to show you the door. What that means to me is being the most authentic version of myself I can be. This kind of work calls for an intense amount of vulnerability, something I feel as if I am more inclined to be comfortable with. Why that is, I'm not entirely sure, but it's a gift that's helped me (and what I hope to be others as well) through a lot of hardship. That's why I do what I do.
