Dane Hobrecht is a versatile actor known for his dynamic range and distinctive ability to infuse physical storytelling into his performances. Beginning his career on stage before expanding into film and television, he has built a reputation for grounded, emotionally honest work that resonates with both audiences and collaborators.

His film debut, Richard's Roast, in which he played the leading role of Tom, drew strong attention on the Los Angeles festival circuit and established him as a rising talent to watch. Dane has made a mark in several acclaimed stage productions, including The Lightning Thief: The Percy Jackson Musical (Percy Jackson), The Shape of Things (Adam), and Death of a Salesman (Bernard), earning recognition for the depth and precision of his performances.

He has received multiple nominations from the Orange County Theatre Guild and the Orange Curtain Awards, underscoring his range and growing impact within the Southern California theatre community. With a disciplined approach to his craft and an instinct for authenticity on stage and screen, Dane Hobrecht continues to establish himself as one of the region's most compelling and dynamic emerging actors.
