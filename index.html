Dane Hobrecht is a versatile actor known for his dynamic range and unique ability to infuse physical storytelling into his performances. While he initially made a name for himself on stage, his film debut in 2023's Richard's Roast, where he played the leading role of Tom, has already garnered buzz and earned him a prominent place in film festival circuits across Los Angeles.

Dane Hobrecht has also left a mark in several other notable productions, including Footloose!, Sweeney Todd: The Demon Barber of Fleet Street, and The Lightning Thief: The Percy Jackson Musical, earning critical acclaim for his performances.

Dane Hobrecht was recognized for his "Outstanding Supporting Performance in a Musical" by the OC Theatre Guild, earning No Square Theatre, as well as the city of Laguna Beach as a whole, their first-ever acting nominations. With his unique blend of comedic timing and dramatic range, Dane Hobrecht consistently showcases his potential for significant advancement in the entertainment industry.
